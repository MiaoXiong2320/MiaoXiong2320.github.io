Miao Xiong

I am a PhD student at National University of Singapore advised by Prof. Bryan Hooi . I obtained my undergraduate with double majors in Computer Science and Statistics at Zhejiang University.

My research interests lie in Trustworthy and Responsible AI, especially addressing trust-related challenges in foundation models. I am currently working on topics around LLMs (hallucination mitigation, RAG, constraint decoding etc). I also study Uncertainty Estimation, in the context of Calibration, Failure Prediction and Out-of-Distribution Detection to enhance the reliability of AI-based decision making system.

I am passionate about advancing AI towards greater safety, equity, and sustainability, and remain enthusiastic about embracing new challenges in this dynamic field.
